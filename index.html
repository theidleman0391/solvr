§ 04 / Process

How we work.

A 4-phase operating rhythm, repeatable across any stack.
◉ 01 / MAP

Audit the chaos.

A two-week embed. We sit with your team, document every handoff, and surface the workflows that bleed the most hours.

◉ 02 / DESIGN

Architect the pipeline.

System diagrams, tool selection, failure modes, and a rollout plan. No code yet — just a sharp blueprint you sign off on.

◉ 03 / SHIP

Build & deploy.

Two-week sprints. Working pipelines in production every Friday. Observability, alerts, and runbooks included by default.

◉ 04 / TUNE

Measure & iterate.

Monthly review of failure rates, time saved, and ROI. We tune until the system runs cleaner than the day we shipped it.
