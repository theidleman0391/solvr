The problem.

Northbeam is a marketing intelligence platform trusted by fast-scaling e-commerce brands. Their product was solid — but their customer onboarding was held together by Slack DMs, spreadsheets, and sheer willpower.

Every new account required fourteen discrete manual steps spread across three teams: Sales had to notify CS, CS had to provision accounts in the dashboard, Engineering had to configure API keys, and someone — anyone — had to remember to send the welcome email.

Deals that closed on Friday didn't get onboarded until Wednesday. Customers were churning before they ever saw value. The ops team was spending 38 hours a week just on onboarding logistics — not on supporting customers, not on improving the product, just on moving data from one place to another.

"We had a Notion page titled 'Onboarding Steps (DO NOT FORGET #7)' — that tells you everything you need to know about where we were."
— VP of Customer Success, Northbeam

The approach.

We started with a 2-week embedded audit. We sat in every onboarding sync, shadowed the CS team, and mapped the full lifecycle of a new account — from signed contract to first dashboard login.

The architecture we designed was brutally simple: a single Stripe webhook fires when a subscription becomes active. That event triggers an n8n workflow that handles everything downstream — account provisioning, API key generation, CRM updates, welcome emails, Slack notifications, and a 72-hour activation check.

Delivery timeline

Weeks 1–2 / Map
Embedded with the CS and Engineering teams. Documented all 14 manual handoffs. Identified the Stripe subscription event as the single reliable trigger point.
Week 3 / Design
Architected the pipeline: webhook → n8n orchestration → Postgres provisioning → Slack notifications → email sequences. Zero custom backend code.
Weeks 4–5 / Build
Built the n8n workflow with error handling, retry logic, and dead-letter queues. Staged against test Stripe accounts. Ran 200+ simulated onboards.
Week 6 / Ship + Monitor
Deployed to production. Ran parallel with manual process for 5 days. Zero discrepancies. Killed the old process on day 6.

The result.

Within the first month of deployment, Northbeam saw a 27% lift in activation rates — customers were seeing value on day one instead of day five. The CS team reclaimed 38 hours per week, which they redirected into proactive account management.

The pipeline has processed over 1,400 onboards without a single missed step. Median time from payment to first dashboard login: 94 seconds.

"People sign up at 2am and they're onboarded before the sun comes up. We didn't even know that was possible with our stack."
— Head of Engineering, Northbeam
